About

A Closer Walk was created by Melissa Joyce and is intended to be a helpful resourse to encourage and strengthen others in their faith. If you have been wondering about God and your place in his realm, we hope this website brings you peace.

We want to help address your questions like: "Is God really real?", and, "Does he care about me?" Also, there are links to the Bible, and real testimonials. There is also a Contact section where you may send questions or prayer requests.
